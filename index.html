My Project

EduCare

A Smart Personal Assistant for Students

EduCare is a comprehensive web-based personal assistant designed specifically for university students in Bangladesh. It aims to streamline the academic and personal life of students by offering a suite of intelligent, user-friendly tools within a single platform.

Some Key Features
  • Dashboard
  • Task Manager
  • Study Planner
  • Study Resources
  • Resume Builder
  • Extra Utilities
Project Goals
We envision a one-stop digital space where students won't need 10 separate apps to stay organized and productive
Our platform is designed to be your go-to academic companion, combining study planners, smart tools, and daily utilities in one clean layout.
We believe in simplifying student life by reducing stress, cutting distractions, and making every study session count
Whether you're managing tasks, tracking goals, or preparing for your career everything you need will be just a click away
Step by step, feature by feature-we aim to build a smart, student-first ecosystem that grows with you
